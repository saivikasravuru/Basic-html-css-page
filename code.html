Narendra Modi

☞ Narendra Modi has been the Prime Minister of India since 2014, leading various initiatives to reform and develop the nation.

☞ He played a crucial role in India's economic growth and implementing policies aimed at transforming infrastructure and improving public services.

☞ Under his leadership, the Make in India initiative was launched to encourage companies to manufacture in India.

☞ Modi is also known for his emphasis on clean energy and sustainable development through initiatives like Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and Solar Energy Mission.

☞ Strengthened India's global relationships and influence through strategic partnerships and international platforms like the G20.

☞ Initiated the Digital India campaign to enhance online services, connectivity, and IT infrastructure.
